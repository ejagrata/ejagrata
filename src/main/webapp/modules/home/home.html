e-Jagratha : CM Visit to TCS Campus

"eJagratha Phase 2 activities started off in January 2017 once the schools reopened after the holidays. Phase 2 activities included sessions for aided schools, website creation and Logo and Poster creations.
On 16th January 2017, Hon. Chief Minister of Kerala, Mr. Pinarayi Vijayan inaugurated Phase 2 of the program by launching the website and the poster. All stakeholders including the district administration, financial sponsors and school authorities were present for the function along with the TCS volunteers. "
